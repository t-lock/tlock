Current Passion Project:

Sveltempl (Github) (Demo)

Sveltempl is a small library and toolkit for seamlessly integrating Svelte with Go Templ. It allows you to server-render your svelte components directly within your Golang backend via an embedded v8 isolate, while surgically hydrating them on the front-end. Its flippin' awesome, and paired with HTMX, is exactly the stack I wanted. You might like it too!
